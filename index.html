Hello!

I am Ziheng Zhou.

Having received education in both Asia and the UK, I am fluent in English, Chinese, and Korean, and possess a diverse background that spans architectural design, computing, and digital Manufacturing, reflecting a deep understanding of multiple disciplines and cultures.

I firmly believe that cutting-edge computational thinking & methods can optimize building performance through predictive analytics and real-time data, offer unparalleled insights into situations such as construction progress, pedestrian flow and disaster response strategies, leading to safer and more efficient built environment.

My fundamental principle revolves around the Building Digitalization, guided by three key factors: Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Interactivity. I am dedicated to a research-driven career in Interdisciplinary Areas involving Engineering, Architecture, and Computer Science, with a strong commitment to integrating practical elements.

Stigmergic Spaces - Autonomous and Interactive Building System.(Online)

This project introduces a biological approach to space planning through a multi-agent strategy, underpinned by a powerful algorithmic framework. Stigmergic Spaces addresses the spatial relationships and forceload among diverse programmatic elements. The final spatial arrangement emerges from a competitive ecology, enabling the resolution of intricate design challenges with conflicting demands.

Workshop - collaborator

Digital Manufacture Workshop - Spatial 3D Printing.

This is an experimental project of Spatial 3D Printing. The traditional stacked 3D Printing technology is slow, non-cost-effective and difficult to apply to the construction although it has been relatively mature. The high-precision movement of the 6-Axis Manipulator can be combined with 3D Printing, which brings possibilities of constructing in space, refreshing the logic of architectural design.
